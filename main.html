Best plant for your home

Dracaenas are popular decorative leafy plants that can be cared for even by beginners. Beautiful leaves of rich green color look appropriate in any interior, refresh it and fill it with natural beauty. There are several dozen varieties of dracaena, each of which has its own unique characteristics - from humidifying the air in the room to neutralizing formaldehyde emissions.
It is perfect for decorating houses, halls, offices, shop windows, apartments, winter gardens, terraces, and much more.

In general, dracaena has many advantages: unpretentiousness in care, endurance, chic appearance, and in addition, it is also an excellent air purifier. It has an amazing ability to absorb from the air such harmful substances as formaldehyde, toluene, benzene, which are emitted by plastics, varnishes, paints, linoleum, etc. In addition, it also has bactericidal properties, thanks to which it reduces the number of pathogenic bacteria in the room.
